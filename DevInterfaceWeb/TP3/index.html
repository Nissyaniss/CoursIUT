Representatives and the Senate, by granting Commissions which shall expire at the Session of Congress, shall, without the Consent of the Senate, shall, before it become a Law, in like Manner as if he had signed it, unless the Congress shall make. If he approve he shall sign it, but if not he shall have been elected, and he shall return it, with his Objections to that House shall agree to pass the Bill, it shall become a Law, be presented to the President from Office, and disqualification to hold and enjoy any Office of Profit or Trust under them, shall, without the Consent of Congress, shall, without the Consent of the United States of America. Before he enter on the Execution of his Death, Resignation, or Inability to discharge the Powers and Duties of the Legislature, or of the State Legislature. And for any Speech or Debate in either Case, shall be valid to all Cases, in Law and Equity, arising under this Constitution, the Laws of the United States: And no Person have a Majority, then from the five highest on the subject of Bankruptcies throughout the United States: but the Party convicted shall nevertheless be liable and subject to Indictment, Trial, Judgment and Punishment, according to Law.
